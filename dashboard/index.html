Your Projects

Plant Nanny Project

Tired of forgetting to drink water? Plant Nanny turns hydration into a cute and addictive game. You nurture a virtual plant by drinking water in real life. The more you drink, the happier your plant grows. But beware, neglect your hydration, and your plant will wither away, guilt-tripping you into better hydration habits!

Scream Go Hero Project

Ever wanted to control a game with your voice? Scream Go Hero is a hilarious endless runner where you control a character by making noise. Talk softly to make your character walk and scream to make them jump. It's a mix of frustration and laughter as you try to navigate obstacles while controlling your volume.

RunPee App

Never miss a crucial moment in a movie again! RunPee tells you the best times to take a bathroom break during a film without missing important scenes. It even provides a summary of what you missed while you were away. Perfect for those with small bladders or just needing an excuse to sneak out for popcorn.

Fake Call Plus

Need an escape plan from a boring meeting or an awkward conversation? Fake Call Plus lets you schedule fake incoming calls with customized caller IDs and pre-recorded voices. Whether it's a call from "Mom" or "The Boss," use it to gracefully exit any situation with a believable excuse.

Forest

Struggling with concentration and productivity? Forest helps you stay focused and productive by gamifying the task of staying off your phone. Set a timer for how long you want to concentrate, and a virtual tree will grow during that time. If you leave the app to check social media or messages, your tree will wither. Over time, you can grow a whole forest of productivity while minimizing distractions.

Sleep Cycle

Want to wake up feeling refreshed every morning? Sleep Cycle tracks your sleep patterns and wakes you up during your lightest sleep phase, ensuring you wake up feeling rested and energized. By analyzing your movements and sounds throughout the night, Sleep Cycle provides valuable insights into your sleep quality, helping you improve your overall restfulness.
